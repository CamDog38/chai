Velo Presto.

Vélo Presto is the UAE’s first premium mobile bike servicing brand - making expert bicycle maintenance easy, fast, and accessible. from at-home repairs to in-store experiences, they’ve built a loyal following of riders and triathletes across the region.

chai. delivers Vélo Presto’s digital strategy, ensuring every touchpoint reflects the same ease and expertise the brand offers on the ground. we manage monthly content calendars, design product-focused campaigns, and lead the creative direction of social posts and video stories.

whether it’s promoting a new workshop launch, showcasing community rides, or highlighting seasonal discounts, we create content that captures the energy of the cycling lifestyle. through strategic influencer partnerships and local event alignment, we ensure Vélo Presto stays visible and relevant - both to hardcore cyclists and weekend riders alike.

our work helps position Vélo Presto not just as a service provider, but as an essential part of the UAE cycling community.
